UCSB players in Junior United States Bridge Championship

December 30, 2019

Two of our members have recently competed in the 2019 Michael Seamon Junior United States Bridge Championship (MSJUSBC) Michael Zheng played in the Under 21s category, and Zoë Bowlus competed in the Under 26 Womens (“Rona”) category.
